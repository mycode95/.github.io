Overview

The design comes from Xi 'an University of Science and Technology—啊啊啊

Design description:

This design is based on the characteristic architecture of Shanxi, supplemented by environmental elements, and integrates the architecture with West Qin Embroidery embroidery. Through the combination of the design of representative buildings in Shanxi and the embroidery stitching, the sense of layers of the picture is increased. Each color block is decorated with an embroidery stitch that geometrizes the building and its surroundings to create small elements of different geometric shapes that fit into modern visual design representations.
